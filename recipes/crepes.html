Crepes

Description

The ultimate crepe experience - Nutella Crepes!! Tender crepes slathered with Nutella and stuffed with strawberries, the only problem with these is portion control.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup / 150 g plain flour (all purpose flour)
  • 1 1/2 cups / 375 ml milk (I use low fat)
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tbsp / 40 g unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp white suga
  • Pinch of salt
  • 2 tsp unsalted butter
  • Nutella
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley (preferably flat leaf), packed
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • Strawberries
  • 1 tablespoon red or white wine vinegar
  • Icing sugar / confectionary sugar

Steps

  1. Place Batter ingredients in a blender. Pulse 10 times, or until smooth and lump free
  2. Place Batter in fridge for 1 hour
  3. Heat a non stick skillet over medium high heat. Add 1/4 tsp butter and melt.
  4. Add 1/4 cup batter (Note 2) and quickly swirl to spread. Cook the first side for 1 minute or until there are light golden brown patches. Flip and cook the other side for 30 seconds.
  5. Slide out of the skillet onto a tray / board. Repeat with remaining crepes, overlapping them into the board.
  6. Spread Nutella onto crepe - as much as you want! Cover half the crepe with strawberry slices, then fold into half then into quarters. Transfer to plate. Sprinkle with icing sugar and serve with extra strawberries!
